Community Service

IMSSA Harmony Iftar(2011-2015)

Indian Muslim Social Service Association (IMSSA),plans mass break-fast events known as 'Iftar' 4 times yearly in the months of Ramadhan,which is the Fasting month for muslims,. I will help out in these iftars which will serve 1000-2000 people.Basically,my role as a volunteer was to pack food boxes,drinks and arrange food on the floor mats. IMSSA, also invites elderly folks and needy families to join this mass event.

Willing Hearts Kitchen(2019)

With a group of friends ,I volunteered in Willing Hearts Kitchen.Willing Hearts is a soup kitchen that prepares,cooks and distributes about 5000 daily meals to over 40 locations island-wide.Through that experience, I learnt to be thankful for the food that comes to our tables and felt proud preparing for needy people.

BV Bazaar F.A.M.E 2017 & 2018

Festival for Art,Movement and Entrepreneurship(FAME) is a school-level fund-raising event for disabled kids organised by the Make-A-Wish Foundation.My class raised funds by organising game booths like Laser Tag and a water balloon booth.As a school,we raised close to $12000.
